INGREDIENTS FOR TOMATO EGG RICE

12 ingredients


Produce
  • 3 Garlic
  • 2 Green onions
  • 4 Tomatoes, medium
Refrigerated
  • 3 Eggs
Canned Goods
  • 1 tsp Dashi
Condiments
  • 2 1/2 tbsp Ketchup
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Soy sauce
Pasta & Grains
  • 1 Bowl of cooked rice
Baking & Spices
  • 1 tbsp Corn Starch
  • 1 Salt & White pepper
  • 1 tbsp White pepper
Liquids
  • 2 tbsp Water
Directons
  • In a large bowl, mix eggs, 1 tsp of white pepper, and salt.
  • Drizzle oil into a pot and turn the heat to medium-high. Drizzle in the egg and set it for 30 seconds before scrambling together. Remove and set aside.
  • In the same pot, add the tomato, white parts of the green onion, and garlic. Turn the heat to medium-high and saute together for 3-4 minutes or until everything is soft.
  • Season with ketchup, soy sauce, dashi powder, and white pepper, and mix well.
  • Add back in the egg, and mix for another 30 seconds.
  • Mix cornstarch and cold water together, and pour into the pan, mix, and let it simmer for another 2 minutes.
  • **Depending if you added dashi powder, you can season with more salt and white pepper to taste.
  • Finish by adding cooked rice and the green onion; enjoy!
