“Tempting Food Recipes” website is created to help users find and view different food recipes based on different categories. The main objective is to solve the problems faced by many people regarding cooking. Cooking has been a burden for many people. So, to conquer this problem, “Tempting Food Recipes” is created to help these people. It allows users to learn and cook various types of recipes. It provides the exact amount of ingredients, cooking time and the method of preparation needed. By cooking yourself, you can also avoid food allergies and tolerance. This website allows the users to find various food recipes for breakfast in the morning, lunch midday, and dinner/supper in the evening. It helps solve cooking issues and to make people great cooks one day.
